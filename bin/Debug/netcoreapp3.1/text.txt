Lviv Polytechnic National University is a leading institution in Ukraine that is dedicated to educating students about environmental conservation and sustainable development.
 The university offers a range of courses and programs that teach students how to use technology to minimize environmental impact and promote sustainable practices.
One of the key subjects in the curriculum at Lviv Polytechnic National University is Object-Oriented Programming. var
 This subject teaches students how to create software applications using the principles of object-oriented design.
 This approach emphasizes the creation of modular, reusable code that can be easily maintained and extended over time. 
Object-Oriented Programming is an essential tool for developing modern software applications, and is a critical skill for anyone seeking a career in the technology industry.
